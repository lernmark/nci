Nordic Circular Investments is a project initiated by Nordic Innovation with

the aim to accelerate circular economy investments across the Nordics.

This project is led by Fundforward in collaboration with Mixi and Circular Norway.

Fundforward

is a perspective-leading investor and ecosystem integrator in the circular economy space, combining hands-on investment experience with strategic ecosystem building. Fundforward backs entrepreneurs developing circular raw material loops and critical infrastructure solutions that enable system-level transformation.

Circular Norway

Circular Norway is a leading impact organisation driving circular economy adoption across industries, with expertise in data-driven analysis, value chain mapping, and risk modelling. CN has a strong track record of collaborating with the financial sector – including banks, pension funds, and insurers – to integrate circular opportunities and risks into investment strategies.

Mixi

MIXI is a dynamic ecosystem catalyst based in Helsinki, accelerating the transition to a circular economy by empowering entrepreneurship through tailored programs, curated events, and multi-sector collaboration.
